How it works

Browse and Search

Users search for energy services or products using filters based on location, service type, or product category.

View Details and Reviews:

View detailed info about energy services/products, incl. descriptions, pricing, availability & reviews to make informed decisions.

Add to Cart or Book Appointment

Users can select energy services or products and either add them to the cart or book an appointment for a specific date and time.

Confirm Order and Payment:

Users can pay for their orders on the platform and receive an order confirmation via email or on the platform. The available payment option is COD (Cash on delivery) for now.

Proceed to Checkout

Users review their order details at checkout and provide delivery and payment information, including any taxes or fees.

Receive Energy Services or Products

Users will receive the energy services or products as scheduled, based on the appointment or delivery date and time.

Provide Feedback

This feedback can help improve the quality of services or products and assist other customers in making informed decisions
